Controls for controller:
 - Left trigger to cycle left
 - Right trigger to cycle right
 - Start/B opens inventory
 - Y opens crafting
 - X uses selected item in inventory bar or places a object in your hand
 - A interacts with object
 
Controls for keyboard:
 - E/Escape opens inventory
 - F opens journal (prob wont do this)
 - C/Mouse 1 uses selected item in inventory bar1
 - Mouse 2 interacts with objects
